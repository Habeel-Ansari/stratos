### Featured Product Categories Section

**Section Title:**  
What We Offer

**Intro Copy:**  
Our product portfolio is designed to support critical electrical systems from cable routing and protection to power distribution, grounding, lighting, and communication infrastructure.

**Category 1 - Cable**  
From low voltage to high voltage cable requirements, Stratos Energy supplies dependable cable solutions for industrial, commercial, and utility applications. Our cable range is selected for stable performance, durability, and safe power transmission in demanding environments.

**Category 2 - Cable Management**  
Organized and protected cable systems are essential for safety and maintenance. We offer cable management solutions that help streamline installations, improve routing efficiency, and support cleaner, space-conscious project execution.

**Category 3 - Electrical Bulk Materials**  
Stratos Energy supplies a wide range of electrical bulk materials for daily project requirements. Our offering includes fittings, conduit accessories, connectors, couplings, clamps, reducers, bushings, conduit bodies, and related installation essentials.

**Category 4 - Earthing and Lightning Protection Materials**  
Effective grounding and lightning protection are vital for equipment safety and operational continuity. We provide earthing and lightning protection materials that help create secure, low-resistance grounding systems for industrial and commercial facilities.

**Category 5 - Junction Boxes and Enclosures**  
We supply indoor and outdoor junction boxes and enclosure systems designed to protect electrical connections in harsh and sensitive environments. Our solutions support safety, durability, and reliable performance under challenging site conditions.

**Category 6 - Switchgear Products**  
Stratos Energy offers switchgear products that support safe power distribution, control, and protection. Our portfolio is suitable for projects requiring dependable circuit protection, system control, and continuity of electrical operations.

**Category 7 - Specialized Cable**  
For specialized applications, we provide cable solutions tailored to more demanding technical requirements. This category can support telecom, utility, and infrastructure applications, including project-specific cable needs.

**Category 8 - Cable Sealing Solutions**  
We offer cable and pipe sealing solutions that help protect critical assets from water, dust, fire, gas, and other environmental risks. These systems are suitable for projects where sealing integrity is essential to safety and equipment protection.

**Category 9 - Lighting Solutions**  
Our lighting range supports industrial, commercial, and infrastructure requirements with efficient, practical, and durable lighting products. We help customers source solutions for indoor, outdoor, utility, and area lighting applications.

**Category 10 - Communication Bulk Materials**  
Stratos Energy also supports network and communication infrastructure with essential materials and accessories used across modern electrical and communication installations.
